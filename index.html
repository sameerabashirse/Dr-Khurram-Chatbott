How it works

Same rules across website, staff panel, and WhatsApp

  1. Patient gives consent and selects a clinic slot.
  2. Backend validates date, time, blocked slots, duplicates, and past slots.
  3. MongoDB stores the appointment, token, consent, reminders, and audit event.
  4. WhatsApp messages and delivery callbacks are logged through Meta webhooks.
Privacy and safety

Designed for appointment management only

Limited data collection

No CNIC, passport, card number, or unnecessary medical history is requested.

Opt-out supported

Patients can send STOP, Unsubscribe, بند, or “Messages band kar dein”.

Emergency guidance first

The assistant does not diagnose or prescribe, and urgent symptoms receive emergency guidance immediately.

FAQ

Can I book on Saturday or Sunday?

Can I check an appointment with only the appointment ID?

No. Appointment lookup requires both appointment ID and patient phone number.

Does the chatbot give medical advice?

No. It supports appointment tasks and safe emergency guidance only.
